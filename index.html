About Our Enterprise

Leading the transition to modern, decentralized, and sustainable electricity infrastructure in Nigeria and across Africa.

Executive Corporate Overview

SafeConnect Investments Limited is a premier Nigerian integrated energy infrastructure and engineering company. We specialize in Independent Power Projects (IPPs), embedded generation systems, hybrid energy infrastructure, smart metering systems, industrial captive power solutions, and utility engineering services.

By combining localized engineering expertise with international technical partnerships, we design and deliver reliable, cost-effective, and environmentally responsible power infrastructure solutions tailored to industrial, commercial, institutional, and community applications.

Our Mission

To develop and deliver reliable, sustainable, and innovative off-grid, embedded, and hybrid power solutions that empower communities, industries, and institutions while bridging Nigeria's energy gap.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's leading provider of off-grid independent power projects, hybrid energy solutions, and mini-industrial parks, driving technological advancement, economic growth, and sustainable development across Africa.

Core Competencies

Comprehensive integrated engineering capabilities spanning the entire lifecycle of power infrastructure projects.

Gas-Powered Generation

We design, deploy, and maintain advanced gas-powered embedded generation solutions utilizing world-class reciprocating engines. Operating on natural gas, biogas, LPG, wood gas, and flare gas to maximize efficiency and flexibility.

  • V6, V8, and V12 reciprocating engines
  • Electrical power range from 115kW to 500kW per unit
  • Soundproof and open-type custom configurations

Mechanical & Electrical Integration

Full-scale engineering support covering equipment installation, vibration management, piping, cooling, synchronization panels, protection circuits, switchgear systems, and civil powerhouse construction.

  • Control panel & switchgear synchronization
  • Transformer & Ring Main Unit (RMU) installations
  • Complete engine overhauls & preventative O&M

Smart Metering & AMI

Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI) solutions designed to secure revenue and promote utility management transparency. Facilitating remote operations, load monitoring, and anti-theft checks.

  • Remote meter reading & customer profiling
  • Digital vending systems & consumption analytics
  • Energy theft detection & real-time analytics

Combined Heat & Power (CHP)

We integrate CHP technology that simultaneously captures waste thermal energy from power generation for heating, steam production, absorption cooling, and carbon dioxide recovery systems.

  • Waste heat recovery for industrial process heating
  • Chilled water systems & steam production
  • Food-grade CO2 recovery for manufacturing

SCADA & Smart Grid Platforms

Deployment of advanced remote operational platforms, SCADA monitoring, energy management systems (EMS), and AI-enabled diagnostics for real-time asset tracking and predictive maintenance.

  • Intelligent load forecasting & distribution management
  • Asset health diagnostics & remote monitoring
  • AI-assisted grid dispatch optimization

End-to-End Project Delivery

Operational control throughout the project lifecycle: from initial needs assessment and site evaluation, procurement and pre-installation, to testing, commissioning, and long-term O&M.

  • Needs assessment, evaluation & enumeration
  • Civil construction & structural reinforcements
  • 24/7 Operations & Maintenance (O&M) lifecycle

Health, Safety & Community (HSC)

A commitment to zero harm, environmental stewardship, and host community collaboration in every single project.

HSC Policy & Commitments

We recognize that the health and safety of our employees, contractors, customers, and host communities are vital to our long-term business success. SafeConnect is committed to planning and executing all engineering activities in a manner that prevents injury, safeguards health, protects the environment, and creates local value.

01

Zero Harm to People

Maintain zero fatalities and major safety incidents across all offices, operational powerhouses, and active construction sites by implementing strict hazard controls.

02

Safe & Healthy Workplaces

Ensure 100% of staff and contractors receive job-specific HSC induction. Conduct thorough risk assessments at every stage of the project cycle.

03

Environmental Stewardship

Reduce carbon emissions by integrating high-efficiency gas technologies, minimizing waste, avoiding pollution, and implementing modern hybrid models.

04

Community Engagement & Well-being

Prioritize local hiring and capacity building. Conduct community forums and Needs Assessments prior to construction to align projects with local economic needs.

Measurable Safety Targets

Our operational performance is measured against stringent annual safety benchmarks reviewed by management:

100%

Employee Training

2+
